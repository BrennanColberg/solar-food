We make aquaponic systems for suburban backyards

"Aquaponics" is a fancy way of saying "plants floating in water, and fish in a tank, with a pump circulating water between them." It's extremely water- and space-efficient, taking up less than 5% of the water and space usage of traditional agricultural: an amazing reduction in environmental impact! What's more, everything it produces is fresh and organic; what's not to love?
